01 / THE ARCHITECTURE

DL Fantasy

DL — short for Dex Library — is a personal archive of fiction, philosophy, and mythology, built to hold everything written across the years and centered on the ongoing story of The God Valley. It is not a blog and not a social platform: no comments, no likes, no accounts. Just a quiet notebook that's been made public.

02 / THE ADMINISTRATOR

Mr. Dex

Writer and sole author behind DL Fantasy. Everything here — every chapter, essay, and page — is written and maintained by one person, through Django's admin, with no editorial calendar and no audience metrics deciding what gets written next.
