The Story

In a fractured world shaped by collapsing civilizations, distorted morality, forgotten gods, and weaponized suffering, humanity survives beneath systems it no longer understands.

The God Valley follows individuals trapped between power, memory, ideology, and the terrifying realization that civilization itself may be built upon inherited delusions.

Every empire claims righteousness. Every god demands sacrifice. Every revolution eventually becomes the thing it once hated.
